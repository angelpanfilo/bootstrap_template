Discover rich history!

Guanajuato has a rich and fascinating history that dates back to pre-Hispanic times, with evidence of human settlements in the area dating back thousands of years. Today, the city's rich history can be explored through its many museums, historic sites, and cultural events.

About

Guanajuato is a charming city located in central Mexico, known for its colorful colonial architecture, rich cultural heritage, and bustling atmosphere. This UNESCO World Heritage Site boasts a vibrant arts scene, with numerous museums, galleries, and theaters showcasing the works of local and international artists. Guanajuato is also famous for its subterranean streets and tunnels, which were originally built to divert floodwaters and now provide a unique and exciting way to navigate the city. Visitors can explore the historic center's winding alleys and plazas, sample delicious local cuisine, and experience the lively music and dance traditions that make Guanajuato a truly special destination.
